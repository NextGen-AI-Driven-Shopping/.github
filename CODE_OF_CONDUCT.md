# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in this project a respectful,
harassment-free experience for everyone — regardless of age, background,
experience level, gender, nationality, or any other personal characteristic.

---

## Our Standards

**Expected behavior:**
- Be respectful and constructive in all interactions
- Give and receive feedback graciously
- Focus on what is best for the project and its users
- Acknowledge and learn from mistakes

**Unacceptable behavior:**
- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks of any kind
- Dismissing or belittling contributions
- Publishing others' private information without consent
- Any conduct that would be considered unprofessional or harmful

---

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies in all project spaces — GitHub issues, pull
requests, discussions, commit messages, and any other forum used by this
organization.

---

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior can be reported to:

**Email:** conduct@recommendme.in

All reports will be reviewed and investigated. We will respond within
72 hours. The project maintainers are obligated to maintain confidentiality
regarding the reporter.

Maintainers who do not follow or enforce this Code of Conduct may face
consequences as determined by other members of the organization's leadership.
